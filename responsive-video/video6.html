Mountain Goat Mounts Mountain

Mountain goats seem to have irrepresible desires to mount mountains, researchers find. In a study published last week in the Rocky Mountain Journal, researchers are flumoxed by how Goats named Mountain Goat just happen to love mounting mountains. Researchers are searching for correlations to this incredible coincidence.
